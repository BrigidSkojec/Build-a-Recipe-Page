Emotionally Boiled Noodles

These noodles didn’t choose the boil. The boil chose them.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 box of pasta (any shape—no one’s judging, except Italians)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced like your dignity after texting first
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Crushed red pepper flakes (just enough to feel alive which may be a shit ton)
  • Grated parmesan (yes, you can use the kind in the green can)
  • Salt (for the pasta water and your soul)

Intructions

  1. Boil water in a pot. Add salt like you’re seasoning generational trauma.
  2. Throw in your pasta. Cook until al dente or until you forget and it’s mush—whatever.
  3. While pasta’s having its hot bath, heat olive oil in a pan. Add garlic. Let it sizzle until golden or slightly burnt because you definitely got distracted.
  4. Add red pepper flakes. Don’t go wild unless you’re into culinary self-harm.
  5. Drain the pasta (save a lil’ water like a responsible adult), then toss it into the pan like it's your ex’s hoodie you definitely meant to return.
  6. Stir. Add parmesan. If it looks dry, splash in some pasta water. Boom—sauce.
  7. Eat straight from the pan like the feral queen you are.
